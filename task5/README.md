Cybercrime Research Reflection

While researching UPI payment fraud for this module, what surprised me the most was the psychological manipulation involved. Scammers do not necessarily hack into systems using complex code; instead, they exploit human emotions like urgency, fear, or excitement. It is alarming how easily an educated person can be tricked into entering a UPI PIN just because a caller creates a false sense of panic about a blocked account or an urgent purchase. As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is my reaction to unsolicited links and urgent financial requests. I will implement a strict "pause and verify" rule—taking a few minutes to independently call the person or contact the official bank branch before initiating any digital transaction, ensuring my finances and personal data remain secure.
